Thank you for choosing HX Task Manager!

Here is a simple walkthrough:

  1. By clicking on "Add Task", you can create tasks with personalized
    title, description and deadline.
  2. In the homepage, click on the task you just created. You'll be able to view its details you just set,
    along with 2 options: "Delete Task" (self explanatory) and "Set Reminders".
  3. By clicking on the latter, you'll have a page where you can set up to 5 reminders.
    You can overwrite the reminders set previously. As the name suggests,
    you'll be sent mail(s) as reminder(s) during the selected timestamp(s),
    however, you can get mails only after you've done the following steps.
  4. Set, then Verify your email by the respective options in the menu (one-time).

  5. Set your timezone (one-time).                                       
  6. Go to "Configure Notifications", where you can turn on and off the reminders.
    By default, they are off. You can switch them on-off anytime.
  7. Make sure to visit the homepage to trigger the website
    scheduled email sending after creating all your tasks with their reminders (if any).
